F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the attendance system work?

The system automatically tracks student attendance using facial recognition, ensuring accurate and real-time data.

2. Can students mark attendance for themselves or others?

No, our system prevents proxy attendance by using secure face recognition technology to verify the presence of each student.

3. Is the attendance data stored securely?

Yes, all attendance data is encrypted and securely stored in our database, ensuring privacy and compliance with data protection regulations.

4. Can teachers edit or correct attendance records?

Yes, teachers have access to modify attendance records in case of errors, ensuring data accuracy and flexibility.

5. What happens if a student’s face is not recognized?

In case of non-recognition, students will be marked as absent, and teachers can manually adjust attendance if necessary.

6. Can the system handle large class sizes?

Yes, our system is designed to scale for large classes, processing attendance for hundreds of students quickly and efficiently.
